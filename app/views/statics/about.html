Founded in 2008, the Kairos Society is a non-profit 501(c)3 organization based in the United States. We work to find and empower the young pioneers who will push the world forward through entrepreneurship and innovation

Kairos Society ASEAN is a regional chapter of Kairos Society which aims to facilitate the establishment of the ecosystem of innovators and mentors in the South-East Asia region. Our mission lies in bringing together the best innovators and tech companies in the region, thereby creating a collective ASEAN technology hub.
