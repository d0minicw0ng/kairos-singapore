Kairos Society ASEAN is a regional chapter of Kairos Society which aims to facilitate the establishment of the ecosystem of innovators and mentors in the Southeast Asia region. Our mission lies in bringing together the best innovators and tech companies in the region, thereby creating a collective ASEAN innovation hub.


Established in 2014, our mission lies in accelerating the growth of high-tech ventures, through the establishment of an ecosystem of entrepreneurs, scientists, industry mentors and investors.
