Kairos Society (ASEAN) is an invitation-only entrepreneurial community of the brightest, new generation leaders across ASEAN changing the world through high-impact ventures.


We are the regional chapter of The Kairos Society, a non-profit organisation founded in 2008, which works to find and empower the young pioneers who will push the world forward through entrepreneurship and innovation.
